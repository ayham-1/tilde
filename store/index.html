CamCurrency

Platform: Google Play Store
Install: BUY(coming soon) FREE

Use your camera to convert foreign currencies on the fly!

Have you ever found yourself in a foreign store but you couldn't evaluate the prices presented in the currency? Fear no more, with the all-new CamCurrency app, you can easily convert to your preferred currency on the fly, using your phone's camera!

Open the app, set conversion operator (multiply or divide), and the conversion rate, hit SET to lock in your settings. Aim your camera and zoom as appropriate on the price tag.

If you would like to automatically detected the foreign currency and the conversion rate, consider buying the ad-free variant!

Hover over the images for zoom!
